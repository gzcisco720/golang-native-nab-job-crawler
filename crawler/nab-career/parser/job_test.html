    Mobile Banker - Bayside region

    Apply now Job no: 749699
    Business unit: Consumer Banking
    Position location: VIC, Melbourne, 330 Collins St
    Work type: Permanent Full time
    Location: Aust - VIC Metro

    • Are you a home lending expert passionate about business development? 
    • Utilise your home lending and relationship building skills  
    • Fantastic opportunity as part of our Bayside region

    Were NAB.

    For more than 150 years, weve been helping our customers with their money. 

    Today, we have more than 32,000 people serving 10 million customers at more than 700 locations in Australia, New Zealand and around the world

    We have built our business on understanding our customers and supporting them. We aim to take the hard work out of banking.

    With over 5,000 people, and through our national network of around 600 retail branches and agencies, our people are the face of NAB.

    So whats the job all about?

    We need someone who is passionate about bringing seamless banking to our customers wherever they may be. This could mean their home, a branch, a coffee shop, their office or anywhere in between. Youll spend plenty of time on the road in your NAB provided vehicle and we have mobile technology to support you. With the ability to determine your own hours and prioritise your day, why wouldnt you consider a role that allows you to work with our customers to support their home ownership dreams.

    Youll be responsible for the continued growth of mortgage customers and building high quality referral networks. We believe youve got to really enjoy what you do and bring energy and hunger to work each day your motivation is the key to your success.

    But theres a lot more to the job than that. At NAB we focus on achieving growth and results through customer satisfaction. Youll be measured on your success through being bold and not being afraid to ask the tough questions and explore customers true needs. Coupled with an engaging and vibrant personality youll shine in this role.

    A career thats going places.

    Our customers are really important to us, but so are our employees. Thats why we invest in our people by providing ongoing training and development so youll never feel unprepared.  At NAB, we are proud of the extensive benefits we offer our employees there really is something for everyone.

    The details 

    In return we'll offer you an attractive salary with the opportunity to earn performance based incentives. Youll be flexible and willing to meet with your customers at a time and place that suits them.  Not afraid to ask for business leads or referrals youll be proactive in your approach.

    With a track record of exceeding sales and business development targets, youll have the support of the brand behind you whilst taking control of your destiny and running your own business to outperform your competitors.

    Youll have in depth experience in retail lending coupled with a professional appearance, exceptional communication / presentation skills and a desire to uncover financial needs. This role is highly autonomous and your focus on building strong relationships within your community and surrounding branch network is a must.

    Ready to jump on board?

    To be part of our vision to be Australia and New Zealands most respected bank, to have fun in your work place and to give back to our customers and community please apply online today!

    We embrace diversity of thought, style and working arrangements to ensure our workforce is representative of the community that we serve.

    To be eligible to apply, you must have Australian or New Zealand citizenship or permanent residency status.

    Advertised: 08 Nov 2019 2:00 PM AUS Eastern Daylight Time
